    Communication is about having a great idea and getting it across in time.
    As a freelance writer, I have worked with groups and individuals across all industries: from the arts, to fashion, to beauty, to hospitality and others. They had unique products or fantastic ideas, yet few managed to distil those qualities into words. Often, the key message was lost in translation and time.

    To give you a few examples, I’ve seen English texts translated from Chinese half the length of the original, product descriptions of brilliant electronics that completely turned people off, websites full of mumbo-jumbo, and excellent copy writing that arrived one week after the product launch.

    For anyone with great ideas, or a serious business to take care of, these are real disasters. (I need an analogy about what customers/ readers get is what you write. If you don’t get the words right, they don’t get anything out of them)

    It is my pleasure to help you get ideas across, with the following guarantees:

    To be efficient and on time, always.

    To be concise and precise with words. You do not need further editing for what I submit.

    To be original in ideas.

    Most importantly, to care for what I write. Trust me, it makes all the difference.
